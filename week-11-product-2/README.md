# Week 11: Product 2 - SaaS Tool

## Goal
Ship your second product. Focus on business model (freemium or subscription).

## Choose ONE to Build

### Option A: Feedback Widget
- Embeddable widget for websites
- Users submit feedback with screenshots
- Dashboard to manage feedback
- Pricing tiers: $0 free, $29/mo pro
- **Why:** High demand in the market

### Option B: Meeting Summarizer
- Record or upload meeting audio
- AI generates summary and action items
- Share with team
- Email notifications
- **Why:** Saves teams time, clear AI value

### Option C: Link-in-Bio Tool (Like Linktree)
- Custom page with links
- Analytics tracking
- Theme customization
- QR code generation
- **Why:** Easy to build, recurring revenue

## Schedule
**Days 1-7:** Build MVP and launch

## Success Looks Like
- Live product with users
- Subscription setup (Stripe integration)
- Email landing page for sign-ups
- $1-100 MRR (doesn't matter how much, matters that it's real)
- Public launch post
